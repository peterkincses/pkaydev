Astley Clarke.com

January 2011 - November 2012

London, UK

Platform: Magento Enterprise

Used: Php, Xml, Html5, Css2/3, lots and lots of jQuery

At Astley Clarke, I was lucky to work with talented in-house graphic designer, Sallie Oxenham.

As the sole front end developer of the company, I was given freedom to go crazy and implement new features and it was a great experience bringing her designs to life.

During my time at the company, we went from a site which was a collection of flattened jpegs to a full Magento Enterprise replatform and complete site redesign.

This included significant template work, cms landing pages, email templates, also researching and integrating several third party modules:

  • Brandammo Pronav
  • Idev Onestepcheckout
  • Predictive Intent Personal Merchant
  • Amshopby Navigation
  • Single Signon by James Kemp
