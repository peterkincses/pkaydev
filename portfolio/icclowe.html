Icc Lowe

February 2014

London, UK

Used: Html5, Css3, jQuery

I've recently spent a short stint at Icc Lowe creting several prototypes for one of their clients.

Lacking any specific requirements, the pages were created from scratch, though I did use one of my favourite sliders, bxSlider for the homepage, and the jQuery Social Media plugin from Design Chemical. for the Oracle social stream.

The video player prototype contains quite a bit of event chaining which has been achieved using localstorage.

The countdown would start when the video has finished, at the same time a question and multiple answers would also appear below the video.

Navigating away from the page is also logged so that people wouldn't be able to restart the counter just by clicking on the page.
